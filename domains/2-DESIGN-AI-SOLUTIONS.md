## Quick Reference

### Key Agent Types
- **Task Agents:** Execute specific business processes
- **Autonomous Agents:** Make decisions and take actions independently
- **Prompt & Response Agents:** Conversational AI for information retrieval

### Key Platforms
- **Copilot Studio:** Low-code agent development
- **Azure AI Foundry:** Custom AI model development
- **Microsoft 365 Copilot:** Productivity-focused AI
- **Dynamics 365:** Business application AI

### Design Principles
- Start with business process, not technology
- Design for user experience and adoption
- Plan for extensibility and customization
- Consider integration across platforms
- Apply responsible AI principles
